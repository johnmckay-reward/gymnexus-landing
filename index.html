A Complete Platform.
A Single Solution.

GymNexus unifies every aspect of your operation—from booking and payments to access and in-gym media.

Your Brand, Your App.

Launch native iOS & Android apps with your logo, colors, and brand. We handle the sign-in (Google/Apple), payments, and push notifications.

  • Apple Pay & Google Pay for one-tap checkout.
  • Built-in system for credits, packages, and discount codes.
  • Effortless onboarding for new members.

Automated, Secure Entry.

Powered by Tapkey, our SDK integration allows members to unlock doors with their phone. Access is automatically granted only for the duration of their paid booking.

  • Secure, time-limited Bluetooth access.
  • Full audit trail of who accessed what, and when.
  • Assign specific locks to specific gym "sites".

Your Central Command Center.

Our powerful web-based dashboard gives you full control. Manage users, view bookings, create discount codes, send push notifications, and pull detailed usage reports.

  • View bookings by site and user; manually create sessions.
  • Send bulk or individual push notifications to members.
  • Real-time Slack feed integration for key events.

Engaging, Automated Workouts.

Elevate the in-gym experience. Members can select a workout video during booking, which will then start automatically on the in-gym screen when their session begins.

  • Manage your complete workout video library from the dashboard.
  • Automatically starts and stops videos in sync with bookings.
  • Creates a premium, guided workout experience.
